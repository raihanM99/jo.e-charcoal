What is Briquette?

Briquettes are a revolutionary heating solution that combines
sustainable practices with exceptional performance.
Made from carefully selected natural materials, these eco-friendly
briquettes offer a cleaner and more efficient alternative to
traditional fuels.

Sawdust
We repurpose this waste material to create briquettes,
reducing the environmental impact and maximizing resource utilization.
Wood Chips
Sourced from sustainably managed forests,
wood chips are another vital component of our briquettes.
Agricultural Waste
We also incorporate agricultural waste, such as rice husks, straw,
or corn stalks, into our briquette production. By utilizing these
by products, we help reduce landfill waste while promoting
a circular economy.
